Since 2004

A 20-Year Culinary Legacy

For over two decades, Tuck Tuck Kee has been more than just a place to eat—it's a living piece of Ipoh's rich culinary heritage. What began over 20 years ago as a humble family stall has blossomed into a beloved neighborhood institution, all while staying true to our roots. We are proudly family-owned and operated, pouring our hearts into every wok and every bowl just as our founder did on day one.

Our secret isn't just in the ingredients; it's in the time-honored techniques passed down through generations. From father to daughter, the rhythmic clatter of the spatula and the roaring flames of the wok hei have been preserved to ensure every bite of our signature noodles and fresh octopus tastes exactly as you remember it.

When you dine with us, you aren't just a customer; you are a guest at our family table. We invite you to experience the warmth, the passion, and the uncompromising flavor that has kept generations of locals coming back for more than 20 years. Watch our story, taste our legacy, and become a part of the Tuck Tuck Kee family.

Our Timeline

Chapter 1: The Beginning

A Father's Passion

Tuck Tuck Kee's story began decades ago in the heart of Bercham, Ipoh, with a hardworking father and a passion for serving authentic local food. What started as a humble family-operated stall gradually became a beloved gathering place for the community, known for its traditional flavors and warm hospitality.

Chapter 2: The Wok

Learning the Craft

From a young age, his daughter spent her days helping at the family business. She learned every aspect of the trade—from preparing fresh ingredients and serving customers to understanding the art of cooking over a hot wok. More importantly, she learned the values of dedication, consistency, and respect for every customer who visited.

Chapter 3: The Secret

Recipes Passed Down

The recipes that made Tuck Tuck Kee special were never written in books. They were carefully passed down from generation to generation, preserving the family's unique cooking methods, secret ingredients, and authentic flavors. These treasured recipes became part of the family's heritage and identity.

Chapter 4: Legacy

Carrying the Legacy

As time passed, the daughter took over the business, carrying forward the legacy her father had built. Together with her family and team, she continues to prepare every dish with the same care and commitment that customers have enjoyed for generations.

Chapter 5: Today

Tradition Meets Community

Today, Tuck Tuck Kee remains a proud family-run restaurant where tradition meets community. Families, friends, and visitors gather around the table to enjoy meals that have stood the test of time. Every dish served reflects a story of hard work, family values, and a deep respect for the culinary traditions of Ipoh.

More than just a restaurant, Tuck Tuck Kee is a family legacy—one that continues to bring people together through the timeless joy of good food.
